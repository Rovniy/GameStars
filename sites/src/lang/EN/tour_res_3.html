• The Tournament is held on the EU West server every week, from Monday to Saturday, from 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM.
  • The Tournament format is individual (we form teams ourselves before each match).
  • Tournament participants begin with equal amount of tournament chips (1,000 chips). The aim is to earn as many chips as possible by the end of the Tournament.
  • The players bet with tournament chips before each match. If a player wins the game, he/she doubles their bet, but in the case of a loss the player will lose chips in the amount of the bet.
  • The number of participants is unlimited. If the number of participants is more than 500, the prize pool and the number of winning places will increase proportionally.
  • The guaranteed prizel pool is $500. The prize pool will be distributed proportionally in accordance with the amount of chips owned by the top 10% of players at the end of the tournament.
  • The "reentry" function is available till 9:00 PM on Wednesday. A participant who is out of the tournament can reenter the same tournament an unlimited number of times.

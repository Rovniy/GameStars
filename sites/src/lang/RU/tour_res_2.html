• The Tournament is held on the EU West server
  • The Tournament matches are open from December 29, 2015 to January 10, 2016, from 6.00 PM to 9.00 PM, Moscow time
  • In each match you must bet with chips. When you accept a match, your bet is blocked. When you lose, you lose your bet. When you win, your blocked bet + extra chips in the amount of the bet you made are returned to you.
  • You can buy extra chips by participating in the referral program.
